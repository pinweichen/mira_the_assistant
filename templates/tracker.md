## Status Key
- **Active** — In progress, on track
- **At Risk** — Behind schedule or has blockers
- **On Hold** — Paused, waiting on external input
- **Complete** — Done, pending closure
- **Planning** — Not yet started, scoping phase

## Priority Key
- **P0** — Critical / due this week
- **P1** — High / due within 2 weeks
- **P2** — Medium / due this month
- **P3** — Low / no hard deadline
